Frequently asked questions about Cairn Builder: Focus Timer.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Pomodoro timer work in Cairn Builder?

Start a 25-minute focus session. When the timer ends, take a 5-minute break. Each completed focus session adds a stone to your cairn. Repeat to build your stack.

What does the Stick Guide do?

Your tiny stick-figure guide reacts to your progress in real time. It cheers when you complete a session, celebrates milestones with a dance, and reacts to your breaks too. It's a lighthearted companion that makes focusing feel less lonely.

Does my cairn reset every day?

Yes. Each day starts with an empty cairn. This keeps things fresh and gives you a daily goal to work toward. Your session history is saved so you can look back at previous days.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, completely. Cairn Builder works with no internet connection. Everything is stored on your device.

Does it collect any data?

No. 100% local. No accounts, no analytics, no ads.

How much does it cost?

One-time purchase, no subscriptions or in-app purchases.
