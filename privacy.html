Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The short version

Cairn Builder collects no data whatsoever. Everything stays on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party SDKs.

What data do we collect?

None. Cairn Builder does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or usage data. Period.

What is stored on your device?

Cairn Builder stores your session history and preferences locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and local file storage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

  • Completed Pomodoro session records
  • Daily cairn progress
  • Timer preferences (if any)

You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Third-party services

Cairn Builder does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app contains no SDKs that collect or transmit data.

Children's privacy

Because Cairn Builder collects no data, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Internet connection

Cairn Builder does not require an internet connection. All features work fully offline.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page. Given our commitment to collecting zero data, we do not anticipate meaningful changes.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us through the App Store page for Cairn Builder.
